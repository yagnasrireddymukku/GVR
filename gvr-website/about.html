The business

All kinds of granite & marble, from one supplier.

GVR Granite & Marbles operates under Vikhyath Industries in Uppamaguluru, Ballikurava, Andhra Pradesh. We supply a wide range of granite and marble for residential and commercial work — and we've built a reputation around two stones in particular, Steel Gray and Black Pearl granite.

Whether you're a homeowner doing a single kitchen or a builder sourcing for a full project, you deal with the same people, get the same straight answers, and pay fair, GST-registered rates.
