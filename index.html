Our specialty

Two granites we know better than anyone

These are the stones we move every week. We know how they cut, where they shine, and what they should cost.

Signature granite

Steel Gray Granite

A clean, even grey with fine speckle — the dependable choice for kitchen countertops, flooring and staircases. Hard-wearing and easy to match across a whole project.

Signature granite

Black Pearl Granite

Deep near-black with a soft pearly shimmer that catches the light. A premium look for feature walls, vanity tops and elevation cladding that wants to feel solid and expensive.
