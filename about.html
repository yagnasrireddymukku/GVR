About Chamundeswari

A family-rooted granite business built on honesty, craft, and the timeless beauty of natural stone.

Our Story

Rooted in Andhra Stone

Chamundeswari Granite Industries was born from a deep respect for the natural stone that the Andhra Pradesh landscape offers so generously. Founded by G. Vishnu Vardhan Reddy and K. Sreeram Reddy, the company is based at Murikipudi Village on the Vemavaram Road in Chilakaluripet Mandal — at the heart of one of India's richest granite belts.

What started as a passion for sourcing the finest local stone has grown into a full-service granite supply business with state-of-the-art cutting, polishing, and finishing capabilities. We supply Stone Grey, Black Pearl, Tiger Black, and other premium granites to architects, interior designers, builders, and contractors across India.
